Hello Kitty

With its white ears and pink dress, Hello Kitty is one of Sanrio's most well known characters. From posters, to video games, you can see Hello Kitty anywhere, especially in Japan.
A fun fact about Hello Kitty is that is promotes tourism in Japan. In other words, Japan is famous for Hello Kitty and has multiple restaurants based off of the fictional character.
Hello Kitty's official name is Kitty White. In the cartoon movies, she was born in London. Created in 1974, there have been several theme parks based off of Hello Kitty.
In Taipei, Taiwan, there are multiple cafes based off of Hello Kitty.
Hello Kitty is portrayed as being good at baking cookies and loves Mama's homemade apple pie. She likes to collect cute things and her favorite subjects in school are English, music and art.
